# Research & Papers

This section is for paper reading, literature synthesis, and research question development.

这里不是只摘摘要，而是记录论文如何提出问题、如何设计实验、如何解释结果，以及它能连接到哪些自己的学习笔记。

## Reading lens

For each paper, ask:

- What problem does it solve?
- What method makes the result convincing?
- What assumptions remain fragile?
- What can be reused in my own research thinking?
