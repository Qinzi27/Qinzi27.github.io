# Life Reflection: Slow Growth

Slow growth is still growth.

慢慢来并不等于停滞。很多长期能力不是靠一次爆发建立的，而是靠稳定重复和及时复盘。

## Thought

When the system is too complex, I avoid it. When it is simple, I return to it.

This blog should therefore stay quiet, practical, and easy to write in.

## Connection

The same principle applies to research:

- simple notes become reusable memory
- repeated reading becomes judgment
- small experiments become projects
