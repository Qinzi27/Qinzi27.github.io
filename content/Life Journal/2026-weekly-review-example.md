# Weekly Review Example

## Wins

- Built the first version of a Quartz-based academic garden.
- Wrote initial examples for learning notes, paper reading, and life writing.
- Clarified the publishing boundary between public notes and private drafts.

## Learning

The week worked best when the task was small enough to finish in one sitting.

这周最有效的学习方式，是把任务切成可以完成的小块，而不是等待一整天的完美状态。

## Energy

- Best work window: morning deep work
- Best reset: short walk and simple stretching
- Needs care: avoid stacking too many open tasks
