# About

This site is a bilingual Chinese-English academic blog and knowledge garden.

这个站点不是单纯的展示页，而是一个长期维护的学习档案：把知识从零散输入变成可复用的结构。

## Focus

- Bioinformatics and computational biology
- Paper reading and research synthesis
- Statistics and reproducible analysis
- Coding notes and small tools
- PhD preparation and academic planning
- Selected life content: weekly reviews, fitness, travel, reading, thoughts, and personal growth

## Writing style

Short notes can stay practical. Long notes should preserve context, assumptions, equations, examples, and follow-up questions.

中文用于记录细节和真实想法，英文用于训练学术表达和形成可分享的研究语言。两种语言可以共存，不需要每篇都完全翻译。
